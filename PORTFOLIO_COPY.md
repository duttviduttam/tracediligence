# Website project copy

## Project title
TraceDiligence

## One-line description
A source-grounded AI workflow that turns public research and uploaded documents into an auditable company diligence brief.

## Short project card
Built a deployable AI diligence application that researches companies, extracts claim-level evidence, scores source reliability, flags unsupported or conflicting conclusions, and exports an executive brief and evidence ledger for human review.
