# TraceDiligence Report: NeuroVista Health (fictional demo)

**Objective:** Assess revenue quality, market position, and key risks.

## Executive Summary

NeuroVista appears to have a credible recurring-revenue base and improving enterprise adoption, but the evidence indicates meaningful customer concentration and limited independent validation of management's market-leadership claims. The highest-priority diligence items are cohort retention, top-customer renewal terms, and reconciliation of reported bookings with recognized revenue.

## Audit Snapshot

- Claims reviewed: 8
- Fully supported: 5
- Requiring review: 3
- Unique cited sources: 5
- Average confidence: 84%

## Key Risks

- Top-three customer concentration of 41% creates renewal and pricing exposure.
- Bookings-to-revenue conversion cannot be validated without backlog and implementation data.
- Competitive-position claims rely partly on company marketing rather than independent evidence.
- Retention and churn metrics are not disclosed by cohort or customer segment.

## Open Diligence Questions

- What are renewal dates, termination rights, and pricing escalators for the top ten customers?
- How do bookings, contracted backlog, billings, and GAAP revenue reconcile by quarter?
- What are gross and net revenue retention by cohort and customer size?
- Which competitors appear most frequently in won and lost opportunities?
